Mini Biography: Dr. Martin Luther King Jr.

Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Was a special person who was a minister and a leader in the fight for what is right. Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Was also a writer and a leader for people around the world who wanted to be treated fairly.

Early Life

Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Was born in a city called Atlanta. Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Grew up with a lot of courage and a strong sense of what's right and wrong. Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Had faith and a big heart.

Note: Students can replace Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. With any person they really like. Want to learn more about.

Important Achievement

Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Played a major role in the American Civil Rights Movement. The American Civil Rights Movement was a deal and Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Was a key person in it.

People remember Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. As a champion of peace. Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Inspired a lot of people to stand up for what's right and to demand to be treated equally.

One thing that made Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Special was that he believed in nonviolent resistance. Nonviolent resistance is when you stand up for what you believe in without hurting anyone.

Correction Corner

Some people think Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Only fought for one group of people.. That is not true. Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Fought for justice and equality for everyone.

Interesting Examples

We can learn from history about laws like the Civil Rights Act of 1964. We can also learn about dates like August 28th, 1963. Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Was a part of these important events.

Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Also taught us to look at people and judge them by who they're on the inside not by how they look on the outside.

Words That Inspire

Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Once said, I have a dream. These words are very powerful and they inspire us to dream big.

Darkness cannot drive out darkness. Only light can do that. Hate cannot drive out hate. Only love can do that.

My Reflection
